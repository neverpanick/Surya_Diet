20g PROTEIN
INGREDIENTS
    Green Gram (Whole Moong) 100g (Soaked) Ginger & Green Chili To taste Onions Chopped fine Cumin (Jeera) 1 tsp
INSTRUCTIONS
  1. Grind soaked moong dal with ginger, chili, and cumin into a batter.
  2. Spread on tawa like a dosa.
  3. Top with chopped onions.
  4. Serve with ginger chutney (Allam Pachadi).

Tip: Don't remove the skin. It has fiber.
